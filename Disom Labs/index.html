ABOUT_DISOM

Building Technology for Language, Security & Innovation

Disom Labs is an independent technology and research initiative focused on language computing, cybersecurity, open-source software, and digital innovation.

We believe every language deserves a place in the digital world. Yet many indigenous and regional languages remain underserved by modern technology. Our mission is to bridge this gap by building tools, platforms, and digital infrastructure that make technology more accessible, inclusive, and future-ready.

Through projects such as OlChikiLang, Ethix OS, and Santali Keyboard, we are creating practical solutions that empower developers, learners, researchers, and communities through open technology.

Beyond software development, Disom Labs explores emerging areas including language processing, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity research, operating systems, and developer tools to create long-term impact for future generations.

Language Technology

Programming languages, keyboards, OCR systems, NLP research, and digital language preservation.

Cybersecurity Research

Security-focused tools, privacy technologies, and operating system development.

Community-driven projects designed for transparency, accessibility, and collaboration.

Core Research & Development Areas

OlChiki Computing
